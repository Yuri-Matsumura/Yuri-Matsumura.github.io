Hello, welcome to my website. I am Yuri Matsumura, an economist at the Economic Analysis Office of the Japan Fair Trade Commission. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from Rice University in 2025. My primary research interests lie in empirical industrial organization and competition policy.

My CV is available here. You can also find my academic work on Google Scholar and arXiv.
